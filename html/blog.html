Travel Trends

There is a pent-up demand for travel after the pandemic and more people are eager to embark on a journey ADVERTISEMENT The latest Sabre survey results revealed several key travel trends that are truly game-changers in the industry. There is optimism in the industry as more countries ease their restrictions and more travellers are booking their first trip after years of being unable to do so. Mandatory testing, vaccination passports and quarantine requirements continue to play a big part of travel in 2022. That said, this primarily applies to unvaccinated travellers. Day by day we're seeing more and more destinations reopening their borders and requirements around testing easing or being removed entirely. Indeed, destinations like Mexico and Slovenia have stripped away any previous restrictions related to Covid-19, for the vaccinated and unvaccinated. The USA is certainly one of our most popular destinations since reopening its borders late last year, while we're looking to New Zealand and Australia for long-haul getaways since it reopened to tourists this spring 2022.

Highlights of South India

On this private India trip, explore unique and diverse landscapes in the south of the country. Begin your trip at cosmopolitan Bangalore and continue on to the historic city of Mysore to discover the grand royal palace on a customized tour. Nearby, take a jungle safari in Nagarhole National Park where you can spot a host of wildlife including tigers, bears, antelopes, and elephants. Up in the hills of Wayanad explore scenic plantation trails and lakes on a private cycling tour with a local expert. Afterward, travel to Cochin where you can experience a traditional dance performance by local artists. Next, its time to unwind on the tranquil backwaters of Kerala aboard a luxury houseboat and then spend time soaking up the sun on the pristine beaches of Mararikulam.
